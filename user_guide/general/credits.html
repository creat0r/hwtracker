Credits

HWtracker was originally developed by Shinichi Okada. CodeIgniter is used as the fundation. CodeIgniter was originally developed by Rick Ellis (CEO of EllisLab, Inc.). CodeIgniter was written for performance in the real world, with many of the class libraries, helpers, and sub-systems borrowed from the code-base of ExpressionEngine.

Backendpro is a website control panel made for developers. HWtracker uses it's user authentication, access control, asset management and other features.

Google chart is used to display tables and charts.

FamFamFam icons are used in HWtracker.
